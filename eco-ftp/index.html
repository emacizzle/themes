EcoApprentice

EcoApprentice is an innovative online resource where schools and local businesses
connect to develop ecologically-friendly solutions to everyday problems.

EcoApprentice is both a compass and a meeting place where employers and
students learn from each other. Many of today’s businesses are seeking to employ
individuals skilled in green trades and sustainable work practices. At the same
time, students at all levels are eager to learn how to make their communities
cleaner and more energy efficient by helping eco-responsible enterprises.

EcoApprentice for Employers: You post questions related to eco-challenges at your
workplace; students respond by researching eco-friendly solutions. In the process, your
business is advertised in a proactive way while the students gain real life problem-solving
experience. Together, you build a sense of community, rewarding for everyone involved.

EcoApprentice for Educators: You have one-stop access to a comprehensive list of businesses that want to collaborate with students in solving their particular environmental challenges. These links have the potential of opening doors to internship placements for work-based credits, solution-focused field trips, classroom presentations, and special project collaborations.

EcoApprentice is a continually growing resource. Here, you will have immediate access to post, view, and learn from the results of students and businesses working together to answer today’s eco-challenges.
